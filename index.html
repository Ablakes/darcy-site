BIO

Darcy was born in Los Gatos, California and grew up living in many different counties including Los Angeles, Orange, and San Bernardino.

As a child one of her favorite ways to pass time was to record herself on her cassette recorder while singing to her cat, talking in different accents and funny voices, and mimicking commercials from Saturday morning Television.

Darcy sang in the school chorus every year and performed in her first play in Elementary school. .. During her early college years Darcy changed majors three times while continuously acting and singing in plays. She eventually succumbed to the call within and majored in acting at Cal State Fullerton, which led to a “chance of a lifetime” trip to St. Petersburg and Moscow, Russia to visit the acting schools and Moscow Art Theatre. While in college Darcy performed in numerous plays and musicals on campus and with independent theatre companies.
